Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

1. Who we are

JobCrawler ("we", "us", "our") is an AI-powered job application tool consisting of a Chrome extension and a cloud API. Our contact email for privacy matters is privacy@jobcrawler.app.

2. What data we collect

Account data

When you register, we collect your email address and a hashed password. We never store your password in plain text.

Profile data

To fill job application forms, you provide a profile containing information such as your name, phone number, educational background, work experience, skills, preferred roles, and Equal Employment Opportunity (EEO) data. This data is stored in our database and used solely to fill forms on your behalf.

Job application data

We store metadata about jobs you apply to: company name, job title, URL, fit score, application status, and the answers generated for each form. This lets you track your applications within the extension.

Usage data

We log API calls (endpoint, timestamp, token count) to enforce rate limits and generate aggregate usage statistics. Logs do not contain the full content of your profile or answers.

3. What we do NOT collect

  • Your LinkedIn, Indeed, or Greenhouse password or session cookies.
  • Your browsing history outside of LinkedIn job pages (linkedin.com/jobs/*).
  • Financial information — payments are processed entirely by Razorpay and we never see your card details.
  • Device identifiers, location, or any biometric data.

4. How we use your data

  • Form filling: Your profile is sent to our API to generate answers for job application fields.
  • Job scoring: Job descriptions are compared against your profile to produce a fit score.
  • Application tracking: We store the outcome of each application so you can review your history.
  • Service improvement: Aggregate, anonymized usage statistics help us improve answer quality.

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising.

5. Third-party services

LLM providers

To generate form answers and cover letters, we send form questions (e.g., "Years of Python experience?") and relevant profile fields to our LLM providers — currently Groq and OpenRouter. We do not send your full profile verbatim; only the fields relevant to each question are included. These providers process data according to their own privacy policies.

Razorpay (payments)

Payments are processed by Razorpay. We receive a payment confirmation and order ID. We never see or store your card number, bank account, or UPI details.

Hosting

Our API is hosted on Railway. The database is hosted on a PostgreSQL instance within the same cloud region. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest.

6. Data retention

We retain your account and profile data for as long as your account is active. Application records are retained for 2 years to allow you to review your job search history.

When you delete your account, all personal data — including your profile, application history, and usage logs — is permanently deleted within 30 days.

7. Your rights (GDPR / DPDP)

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete profile data.
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Portability: Receive your profile data in a machine-readable format (JSON).
  • Objection: Object to processing for legitimate interests.

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@jobcrawler.app. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Security

Passwords are hashed with bcrypt. All API traffic is encrypted via TLS. JWT tokens are short-lived (24 hours) with rotating refresh tokens. Our production environment enforces strict origin checks — only requests from the Chrome extension and our official frontend are accepted.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email to registered users at least 14 days before taking effect. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the current version.

10. Contact

Questions or data requests: privacy@jobcrawler.app
