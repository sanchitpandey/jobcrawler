FAQ

The important questions, answered plainly.

Is this safe?

JobCrawler runs inside your own authenticated Chrome session, uses your saved profile, and gives you review controls before submission. It is designed for careful, user-directed applying.

Will my account get banned?

No automation can promise zero platform risk. JobCrawler reduces risk with Easy Apply-only targeting, human-like pacing, random delays, queue limits, and no CAPTCHA bypassing.

How does scoring work?

The scorer compares each job description with your resume, skills, preferences, and threshold, then returns a fit score, verdict, and any missing requirements.
